# Blinkit-Data-Analysis-Dashboard
## Project Overview

This project analyzes BlinkIT grocery sales data to identify trends, performance metrics, and business insights using Microsoft Excel.
The dashboard provides a visual summary of sales performance across outlets, item types, and visibility metrics, enabling data-driven decision-making.

<a href="https://github.com/ShanalThrisa07/Blinkit-Data-Analysis-Dashboard/blob/main/BlinkIT%20Grocery%20Data%20Excel.xlsx">Dataset</a>

## Objectives

1.To analyze grocery sales performance across different product categories.
2.To identify key factors influencing sales revenue.
3.To visualize patterns in outlet size, location type, and item visibility.
4.To present actionable insights for business improvement.

##Project insights

1.Tier 1 outlets record the highest total and average sales, driven by larger customer bases and better store infrastructure.
2.Item visibility strongly correlates with sales — products placed in more prominent locations achieve significantly higher revenue.
3.Top-peUnderperforming categories like Health & Hygiene and Household goods offer opportunities for targeted promotions and visibility improvement.
4.Low Fat items outperform regular ones, indicating a rising customer preference for health-conscious products.
5.Sales spikes occur during festive and holiday seasons, especially in the fourth quarter, highlighting strong seasonality in consumer spending.
6.Newer outlets (established after 2010) show stronger sales growth, suggesting advantages from modern retail setups and improved management practices.
7.Expanding high-performing outlets and optimizing visibility for low-selling but high-margin items can further boost revenue.
